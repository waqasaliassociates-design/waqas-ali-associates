ESTABLISHED 2011

A Modern Vision Built on
Mathematical Harmony

Waqas Ali Associates (WAA) was founded on the belief that human spaces should be designed with the same structural integrity, aesthetic purity, and functional brilliance found in nature. Over fifteen years, we have transitioned from a localized design studio into an internationally recognized architectural powerhouse with offices in Dubai and London.

Our work is characterized by dramatic geometries, refined material palettes, and a commitment to sustainable excellence. Every luxury villa, mixed-use commercial tower, and cultural pavilion we design is custom-engineered to survive centuries, utilizing structural concrete, local high-grade stone, architectural steel, and ultra-high-performance smart glazing.

"Architecture is the physical manifestation of human ambition. We do not just build shelter; we build the monuments of our clients' legacies."

— Waqas Ali, Principal Architect

BIM Technology

We utilize advanced Building Information Modeling (BIM) to coordinate architecture, engineering, and climate simulation with flawless digital precision.

OUR JOURNEY

A Legacy of Milestones

Fifteen years of continuous growth, ground-breaking designs, and prestigious global commissions.

2011

The Conception of WAA

Principal Architect Waqas Ali founds the boutique design studio in London, focus-driven to create bespoke residential estates that elevate contemporary architecture.

2015

First High-Rise Commission

We expand our operations by designing the Aurelia Plaza, a fifteen-story sustainable commercial center that marks our major entry into commercial developer markets.

2019

Winner of Global Eco-Design Awards

Our Vera Eco-Pavilion is awarded the European Smart Cities Certificate for combining smart graywater systems, active solar canopies, and beautiful local timber screens.

2022

Dubai Headquarters Opening

Opening a primary headquarters office in Dubai, positioning our studio at the heart of fast-developing super-luxury residential architecture across the Middle East.

2026

Net-Zero Urbanism Division

WAA launches a dedicated consultancy team for Carbon-Neutral Urban Environments, working alongside public institutions to map next-generation energy-independent habitats.
