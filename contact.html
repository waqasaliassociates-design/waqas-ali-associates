COMMON INQUIRIES

Frequently Asked Questions

Examine core responses detailing our consulting frameworks, geographical footprint, and design pricing rules.

WAA operates globally. While our primary design studio is located in Dubai, UAE, we actively manage high-end commissions, custom coastal estates, and commercial landmarks throughout the Middle East, Western Europe (primarily London and Munich), North America (California/New York), and tropical resort locations in Southeast Asia. We are equipped with dual structural compliance specialists who adjust all layouts to match local municipal zoning and building codes.

Our consulting fees are structured depending on the scale and complexity of the build. We operate on a hybrid framework combining a flat-rate conceptual modeling phase followed by a standard percentage of the overall construction cost (typically 8% to 12% for ultra-luxury custom residential and 6% to 9% for massive commercial landmarks). Complete itemized fee blueprints are supplied after the initial site and topography consultations.

We integrate sustainability directly into the baseline layout, rather than as an add-on decoration. Every WAA design is plotted using advanced sun-path analysis to maximize natural lighting while leveraging structural overhanging screens and shading systems to prevent excessive thermal loads. We integrate geothermal heat loops, greywater recycling filters, smart ventilation ducts, and active solar tracking canopy systems to achieve A+++ carbon-neutral statuses.

Bespoke custom residential villas (GFA 5,000 to 15,000 SQFT) typically require 14 to 24 months, depending on cliffside or shoreline geological stability. Large-scale corporate skyscrapers, premium shopping centers, or multi-family complexes require 24 to 36 months. This schedule covers the initial structural analysis (2 months), digital BIM drafting and approvals (3-4 months), raw excavation and concrete framing (8-12 months), and advanced interior joinery curation (4-6 months).
