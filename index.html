WHO WE ARE

Where Bold Vision Meets Flawless Precision

At Waqas Ali Associates (WAA), we believe that great architecture is more than just erecting beautiful structures. It is about creating immersive spaces that elevate the human spirit, redefine luxury, and seamlessly blend with their environmental surroundings.

Founded in 2011, our studio has expanded from a boutique residential design house to a full-scale architectural and consultancy firm, serving ultra-high-net-worth clients and visionary developers across the globe.
